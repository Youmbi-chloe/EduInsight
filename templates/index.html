EduInsight

EduInsight is a web-based application developed to collect, store, and analyze student academic and lifestyle data in an organized way. Through an online form, users can submit information such as study habits, attendance, assessment results, sleep patterns, and academic outcomes. The platform then transforms this data into descriptive statistics, charts, and advanced analysis results using regression models, PCA, supervised classification, and K-Means clustering, making it easier to identify trends, relationships, and student profiles.
